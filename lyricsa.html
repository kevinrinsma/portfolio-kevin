Lyricsa - Building a progressive web app.

In this project I gave myself the challenge to make a progressive web app. Also I had to implement an API within the app.

In this project I first started doing a lot of research. I needed to find out how API's work and how I can manage the caching of the app. Because caching makes the app available online. Once all the research was done I started making the app and I am quite proud of the restult.

So in Lyricsa you can go to the tab "Find lyrics" and search for your favorite song, then the app will give you the lyrics for that specific song so you can sing along the next time you hear it!
